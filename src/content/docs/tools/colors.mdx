### Orbit Color Palette

The Orbit color palette offers an extensive range of colors, each with multiple shades from dark to light. This palette is designed to provide designers with flexibility and consistency in their projects.

#### Red shades
- **--o-red-black**: Deep red mixed with black.
- **--o-red-darker**: Very dark red.
- **--o-red-dark**: Dark red.
- **--o-red**: Standard red.
- **--o-red-light**: Light red.
- **--o-red-lighter**: Very light red.
- **--o-red-white**: Light red mixed with white.
